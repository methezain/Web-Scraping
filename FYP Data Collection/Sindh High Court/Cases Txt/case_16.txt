Both  the  parties  namely  Mst.  Kiran  Yazdani  (mother)  and  Khizar 

Pervez  (father)  are  in  attendance  alongwith  minor  Shezar  Khizar  and 

state  that  they  are  reasonably  comfortable  with  the  arrangements  set 

forth in the order dated 23.12.2023however for future visitation rights 

following has been additionally consented by the parties:- 

1.  Custody of the minor will be handed over to the father 10 days 
in  summer  vacations  and  4  days  in  winter  holidays  depending 
on the schedule of the minor.  

2.  On  Eid-ul-Fitr  as  well  as  Eid-ul-Azha,  the  father  will  pick  the 
minor on Chand Raat and drop him on first day of Eid at 4 p.m. 
and on  second  from  9  a.m.  to  4  p.m.  however  the  minor  will 
remain with the mother on the third day. 

3.  On  father  as  well  as  grandfather’s  birth  days  the  father  will 

pick the minor at 3 p.m. and drop him at 7 p.m. 

4.  On family function meaning thereby very close family function, 
parents  after  coordinating  with  each  other  in  advance  at 
designated email will decide pick and drop the minor. 

In view of above, listed application is disposed of. Let the file be 

consigned to record.
