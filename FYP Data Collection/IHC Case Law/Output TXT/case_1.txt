This  is  an  application  for  removal  of  the 

names  of  the  minor  children  of  the  petitioner 

i.e.  respondents  No.5  &  6  from  Exit  Control 

List (‘ECL’), which was placed by virtue of the 

order of this Court on 26.08.2019.  

2. 

 Learned counsel for the applicant,  inter-

alia,  contended  that  since  the  parties  have 

arrived  at  settlement,  hence  the  names  of 

respondents No.5 & 6 be removed from ECL. 

3. 

 Respondent  No.2,  in  person,  confirms 

the  position  and  categorically  submitted  that 

he  has  no  objection  to  remove  the  names  of 

respondents No.5 & 6 from ECL.  

4. 

 In  view  of  above,  instant  application  is 

allowed,  order  dated  26.08.2019,  to  the 

extent of placing of the names of respondents 

No.5  &  6  on  ECL  is  re-called,  consequently, 

the  Ministry  of  Interior,  Islamabad  shall
