01. 

Through  the  instant  petition,  the  petitioner  has 

assailed  judgments  and  decree  dated  14.09.2021  and 

19.05.2021, passed by respondents No.1&2 respectively. 

2.  

Learned  counsel  for  the  petitioner,  inter  alia, 

contended that the learned Trial Court awarded maintenance 

to  the  extent  of  children  in  the  sum  of  Rs.3000/-  each  with 

10%  annual  increase  and  Rs.5000/-  for  respondent  No.3.  It 

was contended that the petitioner also filed suit for restitution 

of conjugal rights against the respondent which was decreed 

in  his  favour  and  execution  petition  is  pending.  It  was 

submitted that in the presence of execution petition pending 

for  restitution  of  conjugal  rights,  award  of  maintenance  was 

not maintainable.
