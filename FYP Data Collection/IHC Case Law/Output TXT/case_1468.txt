Through the instant writ petition, the petitioners 

have  prayed  for  issuance  of  direction  to  respondent 

No.2/NADRA  for  renewal  of  their  Pakistan  Origin  Cards 

(POCs). 

2.   

Learned  counsel  for  respondent  No.1  contends 

that  respondent  No.1  has  processed  case  of 

the 

petitioners/minors before the relevant authority and even 

application  as  well  as  necessary  documents  have  been 

submitted for renewal of their POCs. 

3.   

In  view  of  the  statement  recorded  by  the 

counsel  for  respondent  No.1,  learned  counsel  for  the 

petitioners is directed to  liaison with respondent’s side to 

fulfill  any  further  requirement  for  renewal  of  petitioners’ 

POCs. 

4.   

At this stage, the Law Officer, NADRA contends 

that  as  and  when  the  passports  of  the  minors  got 

renewed by the concerned Embassy, the renewed POCs to 

the petitioners/minors would be issued in accordance with 

Rules and Regulation of the NADRA. 

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
5.   

In view of above, the parties are bound by their 

statements  for  completion  of  process  for  issuance  of 

renewed POCs to the petitioners/minors. 

6.   

Instant  writ  petition  stands  disposed  of,  in 

above terms.
