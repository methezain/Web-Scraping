21.02.2022  Ms. Saira Khalid Rajput, Advocate for the petitioner. 

Learned  counsel  for  the  petitioner  stated  that  the  petitioner 

being  the  real  maternal  aunt  of  the  minor  Muhammad  Ahtisham  and 

being Ex-wife of the respondent No.3, seeks implementation of the order 

dated  02.02.2019  passed  by  the  learned  Family  Judge-West,  Islamabad, 

whereby  the  petitioner  was  allowed  to  meet  with  the  minor  on  the  1st 

and 3rd Saturday of each month.  

The  learned  counsel  further  contended  that  the  said  order 

was  never  implemented  in  letter  and  spirit.  As  per  record,  once  the 

petitioner was allowed to meet with the minor.  

Learned counsel further contended that under section 352 of 

the  Muhammadan  Law,  the  petitioner  being  the  maternal  aunt  has  all 

rights to meet with the minor.  

Learned counsel for the petitioner relied upon the judgments 

of the apex court reported as 2018 SCMR 590 and 2005 CLC 1755.  

Let pre-admission notice be issued to the respondents.
